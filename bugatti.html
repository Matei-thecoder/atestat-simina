Bugatti

Art, Speed, and Engineering Since 1909

About Bugatti

Founded by Ettore Bugatti in Molsheim, France in 1909, Bugatti is a name synonymous with luxury, speed, and avant-garde design. Known for producing some of the fastest and most exclusive hypercars in the world, Bugatti continues to push the boundaries of engineering.

Bugatti’s modern renaissance began with the Veyron, a 1,001-hp engineering marvel. It was followed by the Chiron, Divo, and Bolide — each one a testament to performance and luxury. With limited production and unmatched craftsmanship, every Bugatti is a work of art.

Blending heritage with high technology, Bugatti hypercars are not just fast — they redefine what’s mechanically and aesthetically possible.
