About Ferrari

Ferrari was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 out of Alfa Romeo's race division. Headquartered in Maranello, Italy, Ferrari is one of the world's most powerful and prestigious brands, synonymous with speed, luxury, and heritage.

Known for its dominance in Formula 1 and road cars that blend art with performance, Ferrari continues to lead innovation in automotive design and engineering. Every Ferrari model is a statement of aerodynamic excellence, cutting-edge technology, and unmistakable Italian flair.

From championship-winning F1 cars to limited-edition hypercars, Ferrari embodies passion, precision, and power. Their legacy in motorsport shapes each road car with DNA rooted in competition.
