About Lamborghini

Automobili Lamborghini was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini to compete with Ferrari. Headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy, Lamborghini is celebrated for its aggressive styling, roaring V10 and V12 engines, and pushing the limits of performance and design.

Lamborghini revolutionized supercar aesthetics and performance with mid-engine layouts and bold geometries. Models like the Miura, Countach, and Aventador redefined what it meant to be a supercar, while the brand's motorsport division pushes the envelope on racetracks worldwide.

The brand continues to evolve with hybrid technologies and limited-edition masterpieces while staying true to its radical DNA: extreme, uncompromising, and unmistakably Lamborghini.
