About McLaren

Founded by Bruce McLaren in 1963, McLaren Automotive has its roots in racing excellence. With a base in Woking, England, McLaren's focus on engineering precision and lightweight design has shaped some of the most innovative supercars of the modern era.

McLaren became legendary on the Formula 1 circuit before entering the supercar market with iconic models like the F1. The brand is known for its relentless pursuit of performance, active aerodynamics, and motorsport-derived technologies applied to road cars.

From the Ultimate Series hypercars to everyday supercars in the Sports and Super Series, McLaren delivers pure driving exhilaration with minimal weight and maximum agility.
