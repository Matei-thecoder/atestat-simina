About Pagani

Pagani Automobili was founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani, an Argentinian-born engineer. Based in Modena, Italy, Pagani quickly gained a reputation for crafting hypercars that are both mechanical marvels and pieces of modern art.

Using aerospace materials like carbon-titanium, Pagani cars are built with obsessive attention to detail. Every screw, every surface, every piece is bespoke. Pagani doesn't just build cars â€” it creates rolling sculptures with soul and speed.

With limited production numbers and extreme personalization, Pagani remains one of the most exclusive brands in the hypercar world.
